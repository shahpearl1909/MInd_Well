    Educational Materials

    Guided Meditation

    (Self-Practice)


    Meditation helps reduce anxiety and improve focus through mindfulness and deep breathing. It is a simple practice that can calm the mind and promote relaxation, making it a valuable tool for mental well-being.

    Physical Exercise

    (Self-Practice)


    Physical activity is a powerful way to boost mood, reduce anxiety, and improve overall well-being. Even moderate exercise, like walking or yoga, releases endorphins that help combat stress and enhance feelings of happiness.

    Creative Expression

    (Self-Practice)


    Creative activities like painting, music, or writing offer a non-verbal outlet for emotions. These forms of expression can help release pent-up feelings and improve mood, acting as therapeutic tools for mental health.

    Yoga for Anxiety

    (Self-Practice)


    Yoga offers a gentle way to calm the mind and body, reducing stress and anxiety. It can improve flexibility, mindfulness, and emotional resilience through simple breathing and stretching exercises.
